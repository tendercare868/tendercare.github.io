About Us

Many people want the same things as they get older: to stay in their own homes, to maintain independence for as long as possible and to turn to family and friends for help when needed. Staying in your own home as you get older is called “aging in place.” But many older adults and their families have concerns about safety, getting around or performing other daily activities. Living at home as you age requires careful consideration and planning. At Tender Care 868 we offer services to help you and your loved ones continue to live a happy and comfortably life.

At Tender Care, we pride ourselves on delivering quality and competent care. Our team comprises of five registered nurses and two on call doctors. We use the data collected from the registration form you filled out to assess the level of care we can provide for your loved one.
